Undergraduate programs of Harvard University

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

A Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) is an undergraduate degree that typically requires four years and around 120 credits of study in business fundamentals.

Bachelor of Computer Science (BCS)

The Bachelor of Computer Science course curriculum includes theory and practicals, which makes the students well-trained and skillful in programming, software, hardware and network.

Bachelor of Laws (LLB)

LLB (Bachelor of Laws) is an undergraduate program in the field of legal education. You will learn about jurisprudence, different laws, legal procedures and case studies.

Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS)

The course curriculum of MBBS includes subjects such as anatomy, physiology, and biochemistry. In the later years of the course, students study subjects such as medicine, surgery, and paediatrics.
