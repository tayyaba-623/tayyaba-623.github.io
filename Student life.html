Student Living

Students learn better when their living space is safe, clean and attractive. That’s why we work hard to create a positive, comfortable and welcoming environment. We offer high-quality and a variety of food options and living arrangements to ensure you have the best academic experience.

Health & Wellness

Success happens when your mind and body are functioning at their best. We provide a variety of care options, resources and support. Find the care you need to get back to what matters most – enjoying your time.

Safety & Security

From night rides and walking buddies to a 24-hour advice line and a community-oriented police department, we want all students to feel safe. Explore our safety & security resources.

About Harvard University

The University of Harvard is a global university with a reputation for high-quality teaching and research. We help our graduates, staff and students to shape the world. We’re an agent for change, connecting people, innovating and transforming the lives of our students, staff and the global communities we serve.

Residential Life

First-year dorms and upper-level Houses create a small-college experience within a major research university. Your House is where you eat, sleep—and lay the groundwork for lifelong friendships. The Office of Residential Life oversees four residence halls—Child, Conant, Perkins, and Richards, all of which are located in the North Yard of Harvard's Cambridge campus. The halls are Harvard’s most convenient and affordable graduate housing option. Each building has unique qualities, which you can find in the Residence Hall Tour section.
